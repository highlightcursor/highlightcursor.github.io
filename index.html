How It Works

HighlightCursor is incredibly simple to use, yet powerful in its effect. Once installed from the Chrome Web Store, the extension quietly runs in the background.

When activated, it creates a subtle, customizable highlight around your mouse cursor. This highlight moves seamlessly with your cursor, making it effortless to track its position on any webpage, regardless of content or background.

You can quickly **toggle the highlight on or off** directly from the extension's popup, giving you complete control over when and where you need that extra visual guidance.
