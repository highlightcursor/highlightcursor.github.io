Privacy Policy for HighlightCursor

Last Updated: June 14, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the HighlightCursor Chrome Extension ("the Extension," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our Extension.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

The HighlightCursor Extension is designed with your privacy in mind. We explicitly state that the Extension does NOT collect, transmit, store, or process any of your personal data or browsing activity. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Your browsing history
  • Your personal identity (name, email, IP address, etc.)
  • Website content or data you interact with
  • Any other personally identifiable information (PII)

2. Information We Store Locally

The Extension stores only non-personal configuration preferences directly on your local device (in Chrome's local storage). This information is solely used to remember your preferred settings for the highlight feature and is never transmitted to us or any third parties. This includes:

  • Highlight Size: Your preferred size for the custom highlight.
  • Highlight Enabled State: Whether the highlight feature is currently turned on or off.

This local storage is cleared if you uninstall the Extension.

3. How We Use Information (N/A)

Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no information to use or process.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure (N/A)

Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no information to share or disclose with third parties.

5. Security of Your Information

While we do not handle any personal data, we want to reassure you that the Extension operates locally and does not send any information to external servers, thereby inherently minimizing privacy risks.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • By visiting this page on our website: Contact Page
